How to use Tiny Task

Wellcome to Tiny Task.

We are pleased to help you to manage your own time. Please reserve some few minutes to learn how take the best of Tiny Task.



Creating new tasks:

You can add tasks just puting the task name at the "Add Task" field and clicking the "Add" button.

You don't need the tasks anymore? no problem! just click "Clear All" and we will clean the task list for you.

Task list:

After you add your how tasks you can see them on task list.

You've finished a task? Awesome! just click at trash can at the task side and done!
